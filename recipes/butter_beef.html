Butter Beef

A delicious dish that you will surely love it.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds cubed beef stew meat
  • Â½ cup butter
  • 1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix

Directions

  1. Step 1 - Place beef and butter in a slow cooker and sprinkle onion soup mix over top.
  2. Step 2 - Cover, and cook until meat is tender, on Low for 8 hours, or on High for 4 to 5 hours. Stir once or twice during cook time.
